Welcome to Job Centre Australia Job Centre Australia Limited is a not-for profit community-based organisation that has provided services to people with a disability through State and Federal Government funding for 25 years.

We specialise in empowering people with a disability, illness or health condition to improve their circumstances through a range of training, support and employment opportunities.

Our vision: To enhance our client’s quality of life by developing their potential.

Our mission: Empowering people for tomorrow’s opportunities.
